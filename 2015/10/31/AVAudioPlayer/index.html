Func

  • prepareToPlay: preloads buffers and acquires audio hardware, which minimizes the lag between calling play method and the start of sound output.
  • play: start to play
  • pause: does not release the audio hardware
  • stop: stops playback and undoes the setup needed for playback
  • volumn: volumn
  • playing: true/false for whether the audio player is playing
