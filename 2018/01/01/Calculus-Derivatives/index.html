导数和微分

发表于 2018-01-01
字数统计: 781

DEFINITIONS

Slope, Tangent Line and Derivative

The slope of the curve $y = f(x)$ at the point $P(x_{0}, f(x_{0}))$ is the number

$$ m = \lim_{h\rightarrow 0} \frac{f(x_{0} + h)-f(x_{0})}{h} $$

provided the limit exists.

The tangent line to the curve at P is the line through P with this slope.

The derivative of the function $f(x)$ with respect to the variable x is the function $f’$ whose value at x is

$$ f’(x) = \lim_{h\rightarrow 0 } \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} $$

provided the limit exists.

The process of calculating a derivative is called differentiation.

求导数的过程叫做微分。

导数存在的条件是表达式的左极限等于右极限，即函数的左导数等于右导数。

Alternative Formula for the Derivative

$$ f’(x) = \lim_{z\rightarrow x} \frac{f(z)-f(x)}{z-x} $$

Critical Point

An interior point of the domain of a function $f$ where $f’$ is zero or undefined is a critical point of $f$.

Concave Up, Concave Down

The graph of a differentiable function $y=f(x)$ is

concave up on an open interval if $f’$ is increasing, $f’’ > 0$

concave down on an open interval if $f’$ is decreasing, $f’’ < 0$

THEOREM

Differentiability Implies Continuity(可导必连续)

if $f$ has a derivative at $x = c$, then $f$ is continuous at $x = c$.

Intermediate Value Property of Derivatives(导数的介值定理)

if $a$ and $b$ are any two points in an interval on which $f$ is differentiable, then $f’$ takes on every value between $f’(a)$ and $f’(b)$.

若 $f(x)$ 在区间 $(a,b)$ 内可导，则导数 $f’$ 可以取到 $(f’(a), f’(b))$ 的任意值。

The First Derivative Theorem for Local Extreme Values

If $f$ has a local maximum or minimum value at an interior point $c$ of its domain, and if $f’$ is defined at $c$, then

$$ f’(c) = 0 $$

Roll’s Theorem(罗尔定理)

Suppose that $y = f(x)$ is continuous at every point of the closed interval $[a,b]$ and differentiable at every point of its interior $(a,b)$. If $f(a) = f(b)$, then there is at least one number $c$ in $(a,b)$ at which

$$ f’(c) = 0 $$

The Mean Value Theorem(拉格朗日中值定理)

Suppose $y = f(x)$ is continuous on a closed interval $[a,b]$ and differenetiable on the interval’s interior $(a,b)$. Then there is at least one point $c$ in $(a,b)$ at which

$$ \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a} = f’(c) $$

Cauchy’s Mean Value Theorem(柯西中值定理)

Suppose functions $f$ and $g$ are continuous on $[a,b]$ and differentiable throughout $(a,b)$ and also suppose $g’(x)\neq 0$ throughout $(a,b)$. Then there exists a number $c$ in $(a,b)$ at which

$$ \frac{f’(c)}{g’(c)} = \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{g(b)-g(a)} $$

Second Derivative Test for Local Extrema

Suppose $f’’$ is continuous on an open interval that contains $x=c$

  • if $f’(c)=0$ and $f’’(c)<0$, then $f$ has a local maximum at $x=c$
  • if $f’(c)=0$ and $f’’(c)>0$, then $f$ has a local minimum at $x=c$
  • if $f’(c)=0$ and $f’’(c)=0$, then the test fails. The function $f$ may have a local maximum, a local minimum, or neither.

LHopital’s Rule(洛必达法则)

Suppose that $f(a)=g(a)=0$, that $f’(a)$ and $g’(a)$ exist, and that $g’(a)\neq 0$. Then

$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{f’(a)}{g’(a)} $$

$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x\rightarrow a} \frac{f’(x)}{g’(x)} $$

Rule

  • Operation

$$ (u\pm v)’ = u’\pm v’ \; \; \; (Cu)’ = Cu’ $$

$$ (uv)’ = u’v + uv’ \; \; \; (\frac{u}{v})’ = \frac{u’v-uv’}{v^2} $$

  • Power

$$ (C)’ = 0 \; \; \; (x^n)’ = nx^{n-1} $$

$$ (e^x)’ = e^x \; \; \; (a^x)’ = a^xlna $$

$$ (lnx)’ = \frac{1}{x} \; \; \; (log_ax)’ = \frac{1}{xlna} $$

  • Trigonometric

$$ (sinx)’ = cosx \; \; \; (cosx)’ = -sinx $$

$$ (tanx)’ = sec^2x \; \; \; (cotx)’ = -csc^2x $$

$$ (secx)’ = tanx \cdot secx \; \; \; (cscx)’ = -cotx \cdot cscx $$

$$ (arcsinx)’ = \frac{1}{\sqrt(1-x^2)} \; \; \; (arccosx)’ = -\frac{1}{\sqrt(1-x^2)} $$

$$ (arctanx)’ = \frac{1}{1+x^2} \; \; \; (arccotx)’ = -\frac{1}{1+x^2} $$

  • Inverse

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{\frac{dx}{dy}} $$

$$ f’(x) = \frac{1}{g’(y)} $$

$$ (f^{-1}(x))’ = \frac{1}{f’(f^{-1}(x))} $$
